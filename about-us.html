About Us

Local Import

Our impact extends beyond our products, it encompasses the broader community and environment. We have prioritized local economic development by establishing fair trade relationships with farmers and creating job opportunities.

This social commitment aligns with our values of fostering prosperity within the regions where we operate. We have also supported the KNUST Obuasi SRC Food Bank project, KNUST School of Business Food Bank Project-Obuasi and finally, the Muslim community, KNUST Obuasi Campus through our collaborative initiative with SM Foods and Beverages. Moreover, our dedication to organic farming practices not only ensures the health and well-being of our consumers but also contributes to environmental sustainability. By avoiding preservatives and harmful chemicals, we minimize our ecological footprint, promoting biodiversity and protecting the local ecosystem.

Contribution to the SDGs

SDG 1: No Poverty: Ariz Smart Farms's creation of employment opportunities and economic empowerment initiatives align with SDG 1 by contributing to poverty reduction within the local community.

SDG 2: Zero Hunger: By promoting sustainable agriculture and producing organic, locally sourced food products, Ariz Smart Farms supports SDG 2, aiming to eliminate hunger and improve food security.

SDG 3: Good Health and Well-Being: The business indirectly contributes to SDG 3 by enhancing the well-being of individuals through improved access to nutritious food and employment opportunities, ultimately supporting better health outcomes.

SDG 8: Decent Work and Economic Growth: Ariz Smart Farms directly addresses SDG 8 by creating jobs, providing skill development opportunities, and contributing to overall economic growth within the community.

SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production: The implementation of sustainable farming and processing practices reflects Ariz Smart Farms's commitment to responsible consumption and production, aligning with SDG 12.

SDG 13: Climate Action: Through sustainable agriculture practices and a focus on reducing environmental impact, Ariz Smart Farms contributes to SDG 13 by actively addressing climate change.

SDG 15: Life on Land: Ariz Smart Farms's commitment to organic farming and biodiversity conservation supports SDG 15, aiming to protect, restore, and promote sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems.

SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals: Collaborating with external partners, including local farmers, SM Foods and Beverages and AYEN, showcases Ariz Smart Farms's commitment to SDG 17 by actively seeking partnerships to achieve shared sustainable development goals. Ariz Smart Farms's core activities and values align with various Sustainable Development Goals, demonstrating a holistic approach to social, economic, and environmental sustainability. The business serves as a catalyst for positive change and progress within the framework of the SDGs.
